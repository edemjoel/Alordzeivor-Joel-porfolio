I'm Alordzeivor Joel — a direct response copywriter specialised in the real estate industry. I understand that selling property isn't just about square footage and amenities — it's about helping buyers picture their future.

Over the past 2+ years, I've partnered with real estate developers across Ghana and West Africa to craft copy that generates enquiries, drives site visits, and ultimately closes sales. From luxury residential developments to affordable housing schemes, I know how to position a project and compel the right buyers to act.

Great real estate copy speaks to both the head and the heart — the practical investor and the dreaming homeowner. I write for both.
